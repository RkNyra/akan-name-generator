Akan Name Generator

Akan Names are derived from Ghanaian culture. In Ghana, children are given their first name as a "day-name" which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

This application enables you to find out the day of the week you were born and further suggests to you what your Akan Name would have been based on your gender and birthday.
